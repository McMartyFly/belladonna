Handmade Breads and Pastas Prepared Daily

OUR BREAD AND PASTA

We love the smell of fresh baked bread. We also are picky about how our pasta is made. This is why our bread and pasta is made using only the simplest of ingredients to bring out smells and flavors that beckon people from the area. Stop by anytime and experience simplicity at its finest.

Our pasta is reknown for being the freshest around. We never use dried pasta. Each pasta dish is made with the finest ingredients by hand. The pasta is rolled out and milled to order.

*We offer Gluten Free pasta (made from scratch) for those who prefer to eliminate gluten from their diet.

Our Specialties
