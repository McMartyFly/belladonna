This is Our Story...

Affordable Home-cooked Italian Cuisine

Bella Donna is an Italian restaurant and gourmet pizzeria located in Pittsboro, NC. Bella Donna specializes in Tuscan dishes and pizzas made with the area's finest ingredients. Fresh pasta made to order - period.

Cold Beer & Hot Snacks at Our Bar

Whether it's a local craft brew or one of our delicious fine wines, we can make sure you have what you need to wash down your great dinner from Bella Donna Italian Restuarant. Our wine list is extensive. Our craft and other beers will appeal to the beer lovers. Our bar staff is also prepared to mix your favorite libation.

Homemade Pasta & Bread Prepared Every Day!

We love the smell of fresh baked bread. We also are picky about how our pasta is made. This is why our bread and pasta is made using only the simplest of ingredients to bring out smells and flavors that beckon people from the area. Stop by anytime and experience simplicity at its finest.

Our pasta is reknown for being the freshest around. We never use dried pasta. Each pasta dish is made with the finest ingredients by hand. The pasta is rolled out and milled to order.

*We offer Gluten Free pasta (made from scratch) for those who prefer to eliminate gluten from their diet.
